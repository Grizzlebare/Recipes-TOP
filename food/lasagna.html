Lasagna

Description

This is a simple lasagna recipe that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with the onion and garlic.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and egg.
  7. Spread a portion of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Layer half of the noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  10. Top with half of the mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Repeat layers in the same order.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
