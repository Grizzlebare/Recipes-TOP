Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are perfect for a delicious breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Gradually mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients until just combined.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
